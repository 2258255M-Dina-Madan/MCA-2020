Comparing MFCC Histograms

For the three tracks the MFFCs, on first glance, are quite similar. However, I believe they can be used to identify the three tracks on closer analysis. Firstly, feature 0, which is the most different feature indicates which track is which. Track 1 is slow to start with nearly 10 seconds of quiet. This can be seen in the MFCC histogram with there being only a small peak at the end of feature 0. Harati Harati is not only easily identifiable from feature 0, but looking at the histogram as a whole it is clear the audio and graph match. This is because this song is quite loud throughout, with different voices and instruments making it upbeat. Rain Forest Dreams and Harati Harati have quite a similar histogram apart from feature 0. Rain Forest Dreams is slightly quieter to start, but its tempo is concsistent throughout. Therefore, fetaure 0 is the clearest identifer of this track.
